Lotus Temple, New Delhi

The Lotus Temple, located in Delhi, India, is a Baháʼí House of Worship that was dedicated in December 1986. Notable for its flowerlike shape, it has become a prominent attraction in the city.

Darjeeling Toy-Train

The Darjeeling Toy Train, is a prominent crowd-puller in the beautiful hill station of West Bengal with outstanding engineering marvel, noted for being the best example of a hill passenger railway.

Snake Boats, Kerala

Vallam Kulangara - Snake boats Vallam Kali (vaḷḷaṃ kaḷi, literally: boat game) is a traditional boat race in Kerala, India. It is a form of canoe racing, and uses paddled war canoes.

Varanasi

A major religious hub in India, it is the holiest of the seven sacred cities in Hinduism and Jainism, and played an important role in the development of Buddhism and Ravidassia.

CharMinar, Hyderabad

The Charminar, constructed in 1591, is a monument and mosque located in Hyderabad, Telangana, India has become known globally as a symbol of Hyderabad and is listed among the most recognized structures in India.

Dal Lake, Srinagar

It is integral to tourism and recreation in Kashmir and is named the "Lake of Flowers", "Jewel in the crown of Kashmir" or "Srinagar's Jewel".
